Intro

What's it all about?

  • Space flight simulator!
  • Bolt parts together to build a rocket (or spaceplane)!
  • Explore a whole new solar system!
  • Land on planets and its moons!

Intro

Protagonists

  • Kerbals are the astronauts you fly rockets with.
  • Kerbals are little green Men/Women.
  • Their EVA-Suits have thruster-backpacks, which enable them to maneuver in space.

Realism

Real life versus KSP

A couple things you have to keep in mind...

  • Engines always start (throttle 0 to 100%).
  • Fuel flow is perfect and infinitely fast.
  • Engines are very heavy.
  • Ion Engines are incredibly powerful.

Realism

Technical limits

Now a couple things you dont have to worry about...

  • Life support.
  • Communication range.
  • Speed of light.
  • N-Body physics.
